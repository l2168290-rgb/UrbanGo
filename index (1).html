About UrbanGo

Learn about our mission and the technology behind our service.

UrbanGo was founded in 2025 with a simple mission: to make public transportation more reliable and accessible for everyone. We believe that accurate real-time information can transform the commuting experience.

Our system uses advanced GPS technology combined with machine learning algorithms to provide the most accurate arrival predictions in the industry. We partner with transportation agencies across the country to bring you this service.

Whether you're a daily commuter or an occasional traveler, UrbanGo helps you save time and reduce stress by knowing exactly when your bus will arrive.
